05 — AI Caller Blueprint
Research → Call → Track → Follow Up
Every prospect is researched before the call. Every outcome is tracked automatically.
Why personalization matters: Generic AI cold calls convert at 1–2%. Research-first personalized calls convert at 8–12%. The difference is the AI mentioning specific things about their business — their star rating, their voicemail, whether they're running ads.
RESEARCHED BEFORE EVERY CALL
🌐 Website & Services
What they offer, service areas, team size
⭐ Google Reviews & Rating
Star rating, review count, sentiment summary
📞 Live Answer vs Voicemail
Silent test call detects how they answer
📣 Running Google Ads?
If yes — they're paying for leads they miss. Top priority target.
📱 Social Media Presence
Active or abandoned Facebook/Instagram
CALL OUTCOMES & AUTOMATION
🔥 Interested
Auto-text Calendly link within 60 seconds. Tagged Hot Lead in GHL.
📅 Callback Requested
Auto-schedules AI callback at exact requested time.
📬 Voicemail
Personalized voicemail left. Retries up to 3× over 7 days.
❌ Not Interested
Tagged DNC. Removed permanently. No further contact.
🤔 Send More Info
Triggers email sequence with industry case study + follow-up in 5 days.
MASTER CALL SCRIPT STRUCTURE
OPENING (5 sec)

"Hi, is this [Business Name]? This is Jordan calling on behalf of [Your Company] — I have a quick 30-second message about your phone system."

THE HOOK (20 sec)

References their specific star rating, mentions if they go to voicemail, notes if they run Google Ads. "That's exactly the problem we solve."

LIVE DEMO (10 sec)

AI plays a clip of what their phone system would sound like — using their actual business name. Hearing is believing.

CLOSE (30 sec)

Option A: close on the spot. Option B: book a 10-min demo. Option C: send a case study. Always give them a clear next step.

Legal note: Opening line must include "This is an automated call on behalf of [Company]." Call only 9am–5pm local time. Honor all DNC requests immediately. Keep call logs as compliance records.
